Cleft sentences

We use cleft sentences to emphasize any part of the sentence.

.. admonition:: Focus in person

    The person who + information + (is/was) + person

.. admonition:: Focus in things

    The thing than + information + (is/was) + thing

.. admonition:: Focus in place

    The place where + information + (is/was) + in place

.. admonition:: Focus in reason

    The reason why + information + (is/was) + because + reason

.. admonition:: Focus in the verb

    What + subject + (do/does/did)+ moment + (is/was) + (verb with to)
